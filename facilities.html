Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply to CMR University?

Applications can be submitted online through our admissions portal. You can also visit our campus in Bangalore for in-person assistance with the application process.

Are campus facilities accessible to visitors?

Yes, prospective students and their families can visit our campus during working hours. For guided tours and specific facility visits, please schedule an appointment through our admissions office.

Does CMR University offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and financial assistance programs for deserving students. Please check our scholarships page for eligibility criteria and application details.

Are hostel facilities available for students?

Yes, CMR University provides comfortable and secure hostel accommodations for both male and female students with modern amenities and round-the-clock security.
