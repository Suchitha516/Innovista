CMR University Overview

Welcome to CMR University, one of the premier educational institutions in Bangalore, Karnataka. Established in 2013 as a State Private University, CMR University is committed to nurturing creative thinkers and leaders of tomorrow through holistic education focusing on research and innovation.

Our university spans across multiple campuses in Bangalore with state-of-the-art facilities, modern classrooms, advanced research laboratories, sports complexes, and comfortable student accommodations. We provide a vibrant learning environment that fosters intellectual growth, innovation, and personal development.

Academic Schools

CMR University is organized into multiple specialized schools, each offering a comprehensive range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. Our schools are led by distinguished faculty members who are experts in their fields and committed to academic excellence.

School of Engineering & Technology

Offering programs in Computer Science, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Electronics, and various Engineering disciplines. The school features cutting-edge laboratories and research facilities.

School of Management

Providing programs in Business Administration, Finance, Marketing, Human Resources, and International Business. The school maintains strong industry connections and offers excellent internship and placement opportunities.

School of Legal Studies

Offering comprehensive programs in Law including BA LLB, BBA LLB, LLM, and PhD in Law. The school emphasizes practical legal training through moot courts and internships with leading law firms.

School of Economics & Commerce

Providing programs in Economics, Commerce, Accounting, and Finance. The school focuses on developing analytical skills and business acumen through case studies and industry exposure.

School of Architecture

Offering programs in Architecture and Design with a focus on sustainable and innovative architectural practices. The school features design studios and digital fabrication labs.

School of Science Studies

Housing programs in various science disciplines including Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Life Sciences. The school emphasizes research and practical training.

Academic Programs

CMR University offers a wide spectrum of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs designed to prepare students for successful careers and academic pursuits. Our curriculum is regularly updated to align with industry requirements and academic advancements.

Undergraduate Programs

We offer numerous undergraduate degree programs across various disciplines. All our undergraduate programs include a foundation curriculum ensuring students receive a well-rounded education alongside specialized knowledge.

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate programs are designed for students seeking to deepen their expertise and specialize in their chosen fields. We offer both thesis and non-thesis options for most programs.

Doctoral Programs

Our doctoral programs focus on original research and are designed for students who wish to contribute to knowledge advancement in their fields. PhD students work closely with faculty mentors on cutting-edge research projects.

Certificate & Diploma Programs

We also offer various certificate and diploma programs for professionals looking to enhance their skills or transition to new career paths. These programs are flexible and can be completed on a part-time basis.

Our History

CMR University stands on the strong foundation of the CMR Group of Institutions, which has been a pioneer in education since 1990. The university was established in 2013 as a State Private University in Karnataka, continuing the legacy of educational excellence.

1990

Establishment of the CMR Group of Institutions with the founding of CMR Institute of Technology.

2000

Expansion of CMR Group with the establishment of multiple educational institutions across Bangalore.

2013

CMR University established as a State Private University through the CMR University Act 2013.

2015

Expansion of academic programs with the introduction of School of Legal Studies and School of Architecture.

2018

Inauguration of the OMBR Campus, expanding the university's infrastructure and facilities.

2020

Introduction of new-age programs in AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science to meet industry demands.

2025

Present day: Continuing our tradition of excellence with enhanced facilities, innovative programs, and global partnerships.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CMR University, admissions process, campus life, and more.

What are the admission requirements? +

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, we consider academic performance in qualifying examinations, entrance test scores (CMRUAT or national-level tests like JEE, NEET, CLAT as applicable), and personal interviews. International students may have additional requirements such as English proficiency tests (IELTS/TOEFL).

Are scholarships available? +

Yes, CMR University offers various scholarships based on academic merit, sports excellence, and financial need. We have special scholarships for women in STEM fields and for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Our Financial Aid Office provides detailed information on available scholarships and application procedures.

What is campus housing like? +

CMR University offers modern hostel facilities for both male and female students across our campuses. The hostels feature comfortable rooms, Wi-Fi, dining facilities, recreation areas, study spaces, and 24/7 security. We provide different room configurations including single, double, and triple sharing options to suit various preferences and budgets.

What career services are available? +

Our Center for Career Development offers comprehensive career services including resume building, interview preparation, career counseling, industry-specific workshops, job fairs, and recruitment drives. We have tie-ups with over 500 companies that regularly recruit our graduates. Our strong industry connections ensure excellent internship and placement opportunities for our students.

Are there opportunities for research? +

Absolutely! CMR University strongly emphasizes research across all disciplines. We have dedicated research centers focusing on areas like Artificial Intelligence, Sustainable Development, Legal Studies, and more. Students can participate in faculty-led research projects, apply for research grants, and present their work at national and international conferences. Our Research Innovation Council provides guidance and support for student researchers.
